    Vulnerability Index Data Info

    This vulnerability index is made with ACS and DAC data.

    American Community Survey (ACS): 2020–2024 ACS 5-year census tract data joined to 2024 TIGER/Line census tract geometry for New York City. The fields used were: noncitizen_pop (count of people who are not U.S. citizens) and noncitizen_lep (count of non-citizens with limited English proficiency). These fields are normalized by population and area (density).

    Disadvantaged Communities (DAC): 2023 NY State data of environmentally and socially disadvantaged areas based on the NY Climate Justice Working Group’s criteria. Fields used were vulnerability_score (social vulnerability) and lmi_poverty_federal (population below 100% of the federal poverty line).

    ACS counts were normalized by population and area. All fields were weighted and then combined to create a vulnerability index. Non-citizen fields received higher weights than the DAC criteria.

    Weights used:

    • noncitizen_pop: 0.10
    • noncitizen_per_km2: 0.10
    • noncitizen_pct_pop: 0.10
    • noncitizen_lep: 0.15
    • noncitizen_lep_per_km2: 0.15
    • noncitizen_lep_pct_pop: 0.10
    • DAC social vulnerability_score: 0.20
    • lmi_poverty_federal: 0.10
